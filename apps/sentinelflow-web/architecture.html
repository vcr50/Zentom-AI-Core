Five Gates to Production

No LLM action touches production without mandatory passage through five sequential governance gates.

1

Risk Evaluation

Every action must pass a quantified risk assessment measuring Technical Severity, Business Impact, Blast Radius, and Operational Context.

Risk Score → 4 Dimensions
2

Policy Validation

The final action must be cleared by the Policy Engine against hard gates. Production systems always trigger escalation.

confidence < 80% → human prod system → escalate high risk → block
3

Confidence & AI Guardian

Autonomous execution is blocked when confidence < 80%, Revenue Risk > $50K, or the action involves irreversible data changes.

confidence < 80% → BLOCK revenue > $50K → BLOCK irreversible → BLOCK
4

Replay Logging

Every step of the decision lifecycle is logged — context, prompt/response, risk scores, policy outcomes, final action.

Full trace recorded
5

Execution

The Execution Controller manages pre-execution checks, sequencing, and step verification before dispatching via Agentforce.

Circuit breaker check Step-by-step verify Rollback on failure
