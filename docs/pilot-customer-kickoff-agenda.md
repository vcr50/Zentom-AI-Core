# Pilot Customer Kickoff Agenda

## 1. Meeting Purpose
To formally align the customer's operations team and SentinelFlow engineering on the objectives, scope, and day-to-day mechanics of the Beta 2 Pilot.

## 2. Target Attendees
- **Customer:** Enterprise Operations Leader, Lead Salesforce Architect, Pilot Admin Users
- **SentinelFlow:** Technical Account Manager (TAM), Lead Solutions Architect, Product Manager

## 3. Agenda (60 Minutes)

### A. Welcome & Introductions (5 mins)
- Meet the dedicated support and product teams.
- Confirm final GO decision from the re-demo phase.

### B. Pilot Scope & Sandbox Overview (10 mins)
- Review the `pilot-handover-package.md`.
- Reiterate what is *in-scope* (FLOW_FAILURE tracing, human approvals, AI Explanation Panel) and what is *out-of-scope* (auto-healing execution).
- Confirm the target environment (`astrosoft` Sandbox) is fully provisioned.

### C. Day 1 Runbook & Scenario Walkthrough (20 mins)
- Walk through the 4 core test scenarios (Standard Failure, Trigger Exception, Human Approval, Telemetry Monitoring).
- Highlight the exact UI locations for the AI Explanation Panel and the Command Center AI Signal preview.

### D. Roles, Responsibilities & Governance (10 mins)
- Reiterate the core governance model: *"AI explains and recommends. Policy controls action. Human approval controls execution."*
- Define the expectations for the Customer Pilot Admin (managing approvals, monitoring dashboards).

### E. Support, Escalation & Feedback (10 mins)
- Introduce the `#sentinelflow-pilot-support` Slack channel.
- Review SLAs for P1/P2 incidents.
- Set expectations for the weekly check-in meetings and end-of-pilot CSAT review.

### F. Q&A and Next Steps (5 mins)
- Open floor for architectural or operational questions.
- Distribute credentials and officially launch the Day 1 Execution.

## 4. Key Takeaways
- The customer leaves fully confident in how to trigger tests, where to view the AI insights, and how to safely govern the incident lifecycle.
- Both teams are aligned on the exact KPIs required to transition to a production rollout.
