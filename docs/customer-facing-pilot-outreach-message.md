# SentinelFlow Customer-Facing Pilot Outreach Message

## Purpose

This draft is the customer-facing outreach message for the SentinelFlow real customer pilot after Milestone 27B Pilot Outreach Pack.

Use this message to invite a qualified Salesforce customer into a controlled, low-risk pilot before wider marketplace rollout.

## Email Subject Options

- Invitation: SentinelFlow Salesforce pilot
- Pilot opportunity for Salesforce incident intelligence
- Controlled Salesforce pilot for SentinelFlow
- Early access pilot: SentinelFlow for Salesforce

## Primary Outreach Email

Hi `<Customer Name>`,

We are opening a small real-customer pilot for SentinelFlow, a Salesforce incident intelligence and approval workflow built to help teams review operational signals, understand recommended next steps, approve safe actions, and audit what happened through a replay timeline.

The goal of the pilot is simple: validate SentinelFlow in a real Salesforce environment with a controlled, low-risk workflow before broader marketplace rollout.

During the pilot, we would help your team test:

- Salesforce package setup and permission roles.
- Hosted Zentom API connection.
- A standard test incident flow.
- Risk scoring, policy decision, recommendation, and runbook output.
- Human approval and rejection.
- Safe Case creation after approval.
- Replay Timeline audit evidence.
- Dashboard and Org Health Score visibility.

The pilot is designed for a Salesforce admin plus one or two operational reviewers. We recommend starting in a sandbox or developer org, then discussing production only after validation and explicit approval.

Expected time commitment:

- 30-45 minute intro/demo call.
- 60-90 minute guided setup and validation session.
- 1-2 week observation and feedback period.
- 30-45 minute pilot review call.

Pilot limitations are intentionally clear: this is not full autonomous remediation, production Agentforce integration, hosted HYBRID Ollama, or a large custom integration project. Human approval remains required before execution.

In return, your team gets early access, guided setup support, a chance to validate SentinelFlow against a real Salesforce workflow, and direct input into the v1.0.1 patch and future roadmap.

Would you be open to a short intro call to see whether this pilot is a fit?

Thanks,

`<Sender Name>`

## Qualification Questions To Ask Before Scheduling

- Do you have a Salesforce admin available for a guided pilot?
- Would you prefer to start in sandbox, developer org, or production?
- Does your team use Salesforce Cases today?
- Would safe Case creation after human approval be an acceptable validation action?
- Are outbound callouts to a hosted API allowed in the target org?
- Who should join from operations, support, security, or governance?

## Notes For Sender

Keep the tone practical and controlled:

- Lead with validation, not hype.
- Be clear that this is a pilot, not a full marketplace rollout.
- Emphasize human approval and auditability.
- Do not promise autonomous remediation.
- Do not promise Agentforce production integration.
- Do not request sensitive data, credentials, or regulated data in test incident text.
