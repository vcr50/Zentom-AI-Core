# Pilot Launch Wrap-up

## 1. Purpose
This document marks the formal completion of the Pilot Launch phase (Milestone 39). It confirms that the SentinelFlow Beta 2 pilot has officially commenced in the target enterprise sandbox and that all monitoring and support structures are actively running.

## 2. Milestone 39 Summary
Following the pilot readiness phase, Milestone 39 successfully transferred ownership of the platform to the customer's operations team. We executed a flawless Day 1 runbook, established continuous monitoring logs, and defined the feedback evaluation framework required to measure the pilot's success.

## 3. Key Artifacts Generated
The following operational documents were created to support the live pilot:
- **Pilot Handover Package:** The consolidated bundle of all technical and operational guidelines provided to the customer.
- **Customer Kickoff Agenda:** The structured 60-minute meeting to align both teams on scope, mechanics, and governance.
- **Pilot Access Confirmation:** The formal verification that all sandbox credentials, permission sets, and external API integrations are active.
- **Pilot Day-1 Execution Checklist:** The step-by-step supervised runbook to prove immediate value during the customer's first login.
- **Pilot Monitoring Log:** The central tracker for daily telemetry, incident highlights, and SLA management.
- **Pilot Feedback Review:** The grading rubric used to evaluate the final pilot KPIs against our predetermined targets.

## 4. Current Status
**Status: PILOT IN PROGRESS (LIVE)**
The customer operations team is actively using SentinelFlow Beta 2 within the `astrosoft` Sandbox. Internal support teams are monitoring the `#sentinelflow-pilot-support` Slack channel and logging daily telemetry.

## 5. Next Phase
The engineering team will shift focus to supporting the live pilot. Once the pilot concludes and the *Pilot Feedback Review* yields a passing grade, the project will advance to the final stage: **Production Rollout and GA Release**.
